Welcome to the Crowd

This is a basketball information / debate website about the NBA It features a stats page where you can find team stats as well as the league leaders in the big three (points, rebounds, and assists). A standings page where you can see the live standings for each NBA team based on division, conference, and league. A Rankings page where you can find our power rankings for teams, our rankings of individual players, and the movement trends describing the rise and fall in specific teams. Finally, a debate page where you as the user can post your own opinions on basketball topics to start a conversation or view other people's opinions which you can reply to in order to start debates. Our goal with this site is to provide information and form a community in the NBA world where users can come to express themselves with other like-minded people.
